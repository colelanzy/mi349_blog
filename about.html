Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Cole, and I am currently studying Computer Science at Michigan State University. I am a senior, looking to finish off my MSU experience the best I can. I love going to Michigan State's athletic events, especially basketball, hockey, and football. Especially with it being my senior year, I am trying to go to as many events as possible! So far this year, I have been to every home football game, one hockey game, and the Pistons' preseason game at the Breslin. I can't wait for the basketball season, it is my favorite MSU sport to watch.

I also love travelling, and I try to travel as much as I can on a college kid's budget. So far, only considering out-of-country destinations, I have been to:

  • Toronto, Canada
  • Blue Mountains, Canada
  • Niagara Falls, Canada
  • Cabo San Lucas, Mexico
  • Cozumel, Mexico

I am hoping to lengthen that list soon, and I am also planning on returning to a few of those destinations this year. So that's a little bit about me... enjoy my blog!
